KINDERGARTEN (2011-2013)

My time in kindergarten was a time full of games, laughter and first learning. It was there where I began to develop skills such as sharing, listening and working as a team. I learned to recognize letters, numbers and colors, while having fun with creative activities like singing, painting and dancing. Additionally, games helped me better understand the world around me and make my first friends. Without a doubt, kindergarten was the place where I began to discover the value of learning and exploring.

  
PRIMARY (2013-2019)

My time in elementary school was fundamental for my growth. I learned not only subjects such as mathematics, language and science, but also values ​​such as respect, honesty and perseverance. At first, it was a bit difficult for me, but over time, I discovered the joy of solving problems and communicating better. Additionally, the friendships I made and shared experiences taught me the importance of working as a team and being empathetic. In short, primary school was the foundation that prepared me to continue learning and growing.

 

   
SECONDARY(2019-2022)

High school was a stage of many challenges and learning. I began to be more independent and have a clearer vision of my interests. The subjects became more complex, but thanks to effort and discipline, I overcame the challenges. Additionally, it was during this period where I forged deeper friendships and learned to collaborate better in a group. High school also taught me the importance of organization and making responsible decisions for my future. It was a stage of personal and academic growth that prepared me for the next steps in my education.

 

 

   
PREPARATORY(2022-2025)

High school was a decisive stage in my life. During those years, I faced greater academic challenges and began to make important decisions about my future. I learned to manage my time better, to work under pressure and to be more autonomous in my studies. In addition, the experiences with my classmates and extracurricular activities helped me get to know myself better and develop social skills. It was a stage of maturity, where I confirmed my goals and prepared myself for the next steps in university and in life.
