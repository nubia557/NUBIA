ADOBE ILLUSTRATOR
Adobe Illustrator is a vector-based graphic design software widely used for creating illustrations, logos, icons and scalable graphics without losing quality. Its versatility and advanced tools make it ideal for designers, illustrators and digital artists. Additionally, it allows seamless integration with other Adobe applications, such as Photoshop and InDesign.
ADOBE DREAMWEAVER
Adobe Dreamweaver is web design and development software that allows you to create, edit and manage websites and applications. It combines an intuitive visual interface with the ability to write code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and other languages. It is ideal for both beginners and advanced developers, offering real-time design tools and support for various web technologies.
ADOBE PREMIERE PRO
Adobe Premiere Pro is a professional video editing software used for post-production of audiovisual content. It offers a wide range of tools for cutting, editing, applying effects, color correction and audio mixing. It is highly used in the film, television and digital content creation industries, thanks to its integration with other Adobe programs, such as After Effects and Photoshop, allowing for an efficient and creative workflow.
ADOBE PHOTOSHOP
Adobe Photoshop is an industry-leading image editing and retouching software. It allows you to manipulate photographs, create digital compositions, design graphics and work with illustrations. It offers advanced tools such as layers, masks, filters and color adjustments, making it ideal for photographers, designers and digital artists. In addition, its integration with other Adobe applications facilitates a more efficient and creative workflow.
ADOBE INDESIGN
Adobe InDesign is professional design and layout software used to create magazines, books, brochures, posters, and other printed or digital materials. It stands out for its advanced typesetting tools, page organization, and compatibility with Illustrator and Photoshop graphics. It is widely used in the publishing and advertising sector, allowing you to create structured and visually attractive designs with great precision.
AUTO CAD
AutoCAD is computer-aided design (CAD) software used for creating plans, 2D and 3D models in architecture, engineering and industrial design. It is widely used by professionals to develop technical drawings with great precision and efficiency. It offers advanced tools for modeling, annotation, and customization through scripting and automation, making it easy to design and document complex projects.
LIGHTROOM
Lightroom is one of the most powerful tools for editing and managing photos. Developed by Adobe, it is designed especially for photographers looking to professionally adjust colors, lighting and details of their images without losing quality. Its intuitive interface allows you to apply quick edits with presets or work in detail with advanced controls. In addition, its cloud synchronization makes it easy to access photos from any device.
MICROSOFT WORD
Microsoft Word is a widely used word processor that is part of the Microsoft Office suite of applications. With an intuitive interface and various editing, formatting and design tools, Word allows you to create everything from simple documents to complex reports. Additionally, it offers features such as spelling and grammar checker, pre-designed templates, and the ability to insert images, tables, and graphs. It is one of the most popular tools in both professional and educational environments due to its versatility and ease of use.
